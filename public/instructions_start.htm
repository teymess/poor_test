Instructions

The HIT has a base reward of $1.00 and a bonus of maximum $1.50. Your total reward will depend on decisions you will take during the HIT.
The expected completion time is 10 - 15 minutes.


The task is divided into three parts:

  • Part 1: A short survey about yourself.
  • Part 2: Reading about and answering questions on a given topic.
  • Part 3: Answering a few opinion questions and providing feedback on this HIT.


Please be aware that:

  • All questions are mandatory.
  • Depending on the answers you give in the survey, we will provide you with personalized information later on. Make sure to answer truthfully to be able to receive the personalized information.
  • You might have to scroll down to answer all the questions on a page.
  • You need to complete the entire study to receive your reward.
  • Survey reponses that appear random or negligent will likely result in HIT rejection.
