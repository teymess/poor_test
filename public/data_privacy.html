Data Protection

Purpose of Data Processing

  • All the data collected in this survey are provided on a voluntary basis and will be stored, processed and used by the researchers listed in Teams page for the purpose of creating "State of Crypto" reports as well as other academic research papers.
  • Any personal data gathered for the aforementioned purposes will be collected, processed and used with respect to the data protection acts of the state of Baden-W端rttemberg (LDSG) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Type of Data Processed

  • In addition to the information you provide voluntarily, we will collect your IP address and other paradata provided by your browser (such as name and version). We will use this information together with the rest of your survey responses solely for the purpose of ensuring that all the data you provided is truthful and uniquely associated with a single human person. After this evaluation is completed, we will delete this additional information forever.
  • If you decide to share your email or crypto address, you may be contacted in the future to receive updates about the State of Crypto Survey and/or invites to participate in future academic studies.
  • Email and crypto addresses will be stored separately from the rest of the survey responses and linked via a pseudonym that only the researchers listed in the Teams page may decrypt.

Duration of Storage

  • The information collected in this survey will be stored indefinitely and may be made available to other academic researchers after the study is completed. In doing so, we will remove any potentially identifying information (if any was provided). Examples of other studies include meta-analyses of similar studies that aggregate information from multiple participants from a broad range of studies. Researchers of future studies will not ask your permission for each new study and will not have access to any information that could potentially identify you, nor will they attempt to do so.
  • Participants reserve the right to access, rectify, or delete their personal data gathered through our studies. Important! If no crypto address or email address is provided, the data is already stored anonymously and can no longer be modified.
